## Your Mission

Test the core learning flow and provide honest feedback on:

- **Content Accuracy:** Are the study materials and quizzes helpful and accurate?

## Testing Tasks (Core Flow)

### Task 1: Study a Topic (10-15 minutes)

1. Navigate to the **Dashboard** (`/`)
2. Click **"START_NEW_STUDY_SESSION()"**
3. Enter a topic of your choice (e.g., "Python Functions", "World War II", "Photosynthesis")
4. Read through the generated study notes
5. **Observe:**
   - How long does it take to generate notes?
   - Is the content accurate and helpful?
   - Is the formatting easy to read?
   - Any errors or confusing sections?

### Task 2: Take a Quiz (10-15 minutes)

1. From the Dashboard, click **"TAKE_QUIZ()"**
2. Enter the same topic you studied
3. Choose difficulty level (Easy, Medium, or Hard)
4. Answer all 5 questions
5. Review your results
6. **Observe:**
   - How long does quiz generation take?
   - Are questions clear and relevant?
   - Is the difficulty level appropriate?
   - Do you see XP gain notifications?
   - Any celebration effects (if you scored 70%+)?

### Task 3: Check Your Progress (5 minutes)

1. Navigate to the **Progress** page
2. View your:
   - Total XP and current level
   - Study streak (days in a row)
   - Achievement badges
   - Recent activity
3. **Observe:**
   - Is your progress accurately tracked?
   - Are stats displayed clearly?
   - Any missing or incorrect data?
   - Do achievements unlock properly?

### Task 4: Submit Feedback (5 minutes)

1. Navigate to `/feedback`
2. Select a feedback category:
   - **UX** - User experience, design, clarity
   - **Speed** - Performance, loading times
   - **Accuracy** - Content quality, correctness
   - **Motivation** - Engagement, gamification
   - **General** - Other feedback
3. Rate your experience (1-5)
4. Share detailed comments (optional but encouraged!)
5. Submit

**Important:** Please submit feedback after each testing session, or at minimum once at the end.

## What to Look For

### User Experience (UX)
- [ ] Is the interface visually appealing?
- [ ] Are buttons and links clearly labeled?
- [ ] Is navigation intuitive?
- [ ] Do error messages make sense?
- [ ] Is the black & white theme consistent?
- [ ] Is text readable (font size, contrast)?

### Performance (Speed)
- [ ] How long do study notes take to generate? (Aim: <10s)
- [ ] How long do quizzes take to generate? (Aim: <8s)
- [ ] Are page transitions smooth?
- [ ] Any lag when interacting with elements?
- [ ] Does caching work (faster 2nd generation)?

### Content Quality (Accuracy)
- [ ] Are study notes comprehensive and accurate?
- [ ] Are quiz questions relevant to the topic?
- [ ] Are answer choices reasonable?
- [ ] Are correct answers actually correct?
- [ ] Is content appropriate for difficulty level?

### Motivation (Engagement)
- [ ] Do XP notifications feel rewarding?
- [ ] Are level-up celebrations satisfying?
- [ ] Do achievement badges motivate you?
- [ ] Does the study streak encourage daily use?
- [ ] Would you use this app regularly?
